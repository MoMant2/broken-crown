Development

The Road Ahead

Phase 1
The Foundation
Singleplayer vertical slice. Core combat, classes, skills, crafting, and the first three zones of the story. Playable 2-4 hour loop from character creation to the first boss.
Phase 2
The Full Journey
Complete singleplayer story across all five rings. Level 1-50 progression, equipment system, dungeons, and the final confrontation at the Worldspire.
Phase 3
The Living World
Multiplayer layer. See other players, trade, form groups. Shared economy and cooperative dungeon runs.
Phase 4
The War for the Crown
Alliance warfare, territory control, PvP zones, guild fortresses. The endgame that gives the crown its meaning.
